About the Show

The Pokémon show is Safe Network's destination for card collectors and fans of all generations. Every stream features live pack openings, graded card auctions, and curated mystery lots spanning vintage Base Set through the latest modern expansions. Whether you're chasing a holographic Charizard or hunting the newest alt-art cards, this is where collectors come together.

HoneyBunBean creates an electric atmosphere where every rip could reveal a chase card and every auction is a chance to add something special to your collection. From PSA slabs and sealed product to one-of-a-kind mystery lots, the Pokémon show delivers the excitement that keeps trainers coming back stream after stream.
