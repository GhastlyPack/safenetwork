About the Show

The Coins & Bullion show is Safe Network's flagship live shopping experience. Every stream features a curated mix of rare coins, silver bars, gold bullion, and surprise mystery lots. DJ Smoltz brings the energy, expertise, and incredible deals that keep collectors coming back for more.

Whether you're a seasoned numismatist or just starting your stack, every show has something for you. From Morgan silver dollars and American Gold Eagles to surprise mystery lots that could contain anything, the thrill of live collecting is unmatched.
