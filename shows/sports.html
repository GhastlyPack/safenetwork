About the Show

The Sports Cards show is where collectors come to chase the biggest pulls in the hobby. Every stream is packed with rookie cards, autographs, graded slabs, and mystery boxes spanning football, basketball, and baseball. MaxoCollects keeps the energy high and the deals real, whether you're hunting a PSA 10 rookie or ripping into a mystery pack live on stream.

Whether you're a longtime collector building a graded set or a newcomer looking to pull your first big hit, this show delivers the thrill of the chase. Live breaks, audience picks, and surprise lots make every stream a can't-miss event for sports card fans.
